Reflection

6 June 2018

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

DBC and EDA appear to take a more holistic and practical approach to academia, compared to traditional institutions. Intensive, communal, hands on study is a big part of their motto, rather than the more theoretical and individualistic approach taken by traditional institutions.

What are your impressions?

I have only positive impressions so far, and I'm sure these impressions will refine and enhance as I go along and my knowledge increases.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I will be fine with it. It's new to me, but I think it will be refreshing.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

No. The bottom line is that it will take a lot of hard work and commitment to succeed.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

Yes and yes! It's a new journey of learning, and I look forward to it.
